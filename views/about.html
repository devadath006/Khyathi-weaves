The Rhythm of Technology & Tradition

Artistry, Innovation, & Authenticity

My journey is an intersection of three worlds. As a **Mridangam artist**, I was raised with rhythmic discipline. As a **3rd-year BCA student**, I am immersed in technology. Khyathi Weaves was born watching my **mother’s love for the saree** and realizing that genuine handloom was being replaced by synthetics.

"I saw a gap where technology could protect heritage. I traveled to Kanchipuram, Kuthampully, and Chettinad to ensure the rhythm of the loom reaches you 100% pure."

We import directly from weavers, ensuring authenticity is never lost in translation.

"Dedicated to all Saree Lovers"

To those who understand that a saree is a heritage wrapped in grace. For the seekers of purity—this platform is for you.
