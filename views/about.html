The Rhythm of Technology & Tradition

Artistry, Innovation, & Authenticity

My journey is an intersection of three distinct worlds. As a **Mridangam artist**, I was raised with the rhythmic discipline of classical tradition. As a **3rd-year BCA student**, I am deeply immersed in the world of modern technology and innovation.

Khyathi Weaves was born from a personal observation—watching my **mother’s lifelong love for the saree** and realizing that the digital age had made it easier for synthetic blends to hide behind the name of "pure" handloom.

"I saw a gap where technology could be used to protect, rather than dilute, our heritage. I traveled to Kanchipuram, Kuthampully, and Chettinad to meet master weavers, ensuring that the rhythm of the loom is translated directly to your screen—100% pure and untouched."

By combining my technical background with a deep-rooted respect for craftsmanship, I personally oversee the sourcing of every thread. We **import directly from weavers**, using this platform to ensure that the authenticity of Indian handloom is never lost in translation.
